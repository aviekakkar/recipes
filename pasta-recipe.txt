Ingrediants: 
	- 150-200g Barrilla Pasta
	- 3/4 tomato's (romano)
	- ample cheess - Parmigiano Reggiano
	- rock salt
	- 1/2 onion

Directions:
	1. Begin with boiling water in a pasta pot (see box for duration)
	2. Add salt generously upon boiling point, and add the pasta
	3. Meanwhile, cook onions and tomatos on low-medium heat on a pan
	4. Add cheess just before introducing al dante pasta to pan
	5. Shut heat source, mix well and serve
